Bangkok (also called: กรุงเทพมหานคร (Thai2English) (Krung Thep Maha Nakhon (“Bangkok — Google Arts & Culture”) population: 11.07 million (MacroTrends. “Bangkok”)) - capital city - most populous city - primate city, next largest city: Chon Buri - population: 1.454 million (MacroTrends. “Chon Buri”), other notable cities: Chiang Mai - population: 1.213 million (MacroTrends. “Chiang Mai”)

Thailand has one megacity, which is Bangkok.

Primate city, Bangkok has almost 10 times the population of the next largest city.

52% urban population (“Thailand Urban Population (% of total population)”)

There are two large and popular airports in Bangkok, Don Mueang Airport (DMK) and Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK). Both are similar international airports, at least in terms of travel convenience (“Which airport (BKK or DMK) to fly out of? - Bangkok Forum - Tripadvisor”). The major train terminals in the city do not run within the city but to places outside of the city, a majority of trains run out from Krung Thep Aphiwat Central Terminal (Thailand Trains).
