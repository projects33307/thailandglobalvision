The arithmetic density in Thailand is 139.9 (“Population density - Thailand”).

Thailand looks to be at stage 4 in the DTM, but on the cusp of stage 5. This is because of the population pyramid which shows a shrinking young population in comparison to a larger middle aged population.

Because of an industrializing economy, Thailand has become a hot destination for migrant workers, especially those in neighboring Cambodia, Laos, and Myanmar. This has resulted in Thailand have significantly more in-migrants than out-migrants (Bylander and Reid).
