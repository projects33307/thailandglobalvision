Unit 1:

Thailand's population in 2021 was 71,601,103 (“Thailand | Data”).

Thailand is 513,120 sq km in area (CIA).

In the North is hilly forests, rice field are located more centrally, the Northeast has many plateaus, and in the Southern peninsula there is jagged coastlines (Encyclopedia Britannica).

Thailand means Land of the Thai/Kingdom of the Thai/Thai Kingdom, and common names in Thai are: ประเทศไทย or เมืองไทย (Thai2English).

Thailand wanted a flag with similar colors to its allies of Britain, France, Russia, and the United States. Red is the symbol of the blood which Thai people have shed for the sake of the country. White symbolizes religion and the purity of Buddhism. And Blue is representative of the royalty of Thailand which carries and guides the nation (Encyclopedia Britannica).
